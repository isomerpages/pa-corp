<p>The People’s Association (PA) has 84 Corporate Members that represent
various sectors of Singapore society, including academic guilds, cultural
and educational organisations, hobby clubs, service organisations, sports
and martial arts associations, students’ unions, uniformed organisations,
unions, youth organisations and professional bodies.</p>
<p>Sharing the common objective of community bonding, PA and its Corporate
Members have leveraged each other’s expertise, strengths, and outreach
efforts to create joint programmes to better serve the community.</p>
