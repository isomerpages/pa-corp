<h4>Welcome Lobby</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Welcome Lobby" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/LKC_7609.jpg">
</div>
<p>Begin your journey by collecting your <strong>personalised RFID tag </strong>to<strong> </strong>optimise
the interactive experience throughout your visit. Discover what makes Singapore's
community building unique through <strong>inspiring stories, interactive quizzes</strong>,
and <strong>mini-games</strong>, and learn how ordinary individuals have
both contributed to and benefited from being part of our community.</p>
<h4>1 | Singapore's Turbulent Beginnings</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Singapore's Turbulent Beginnings" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/LKC_7364.jpg">
</div>
<p>Step back in time to understand how the peace and harmony we enjoy in
Singapore today is <strong>not a natural state of affairs</strong>, but
a constant <strong>work-in-progress</strong>. Experience Singapore's complex
past as a diverse society of migrants with divided loyalties, lacking unity
and facing social unrest. Gain a deeper appreciation for how far we've
come as a nation.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>2 | Forging Our Path to Social Cohesion</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/Picture3.jpg">
</div>
<p>Journey through <strong>six decades of community development </strong>across
three compelling themes:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Unity in Diversity:</strong> See how different communities learned
to live and thrive together</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Cornerstones of Social Cohesion:</strong> Understand how our common
spaces and community initiatives bind us as one</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Shaping Our Shared Identity: </strong>Discover how we foster connections
and belonging amongst neighbours</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Learn how we built the community, fresh out of a turbulent period, and
our independence. Through institutions, programmes, and <strong>common spaces created</strong>,
we turned the vision of a multi-racial society into reality as Singapore
responded to various social challenges and began forging the <strong>path to social cohesion</strong>.</p>
<h4></h4>
<h4>3 | Weathering Crises as a Community</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/Picture4.jpg">
</div>
<p>Explore how Singapore's strong community bonds helped us <strong>emerge stronger</strong> from
various national crises. See real examples of how <strong>opportunities were created</strong> for
Singaporeans to support one another during challenging times, demonstrating
the <strong>power of social capital</strong> built over decades of dedicated
community building efforts.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>4 | Charting Our Way Forward</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/LKC_7575.jpg">
</div>
<p>Reflect on our nation’s <strong>six decades of community building</strong> and
discover how you can contribute to shaping Singapore's future and build
a <strong>We-First society</strong> together. Engage with our <strong>digital Community Mural</strong> and
connect with our volunteers’ inspiring stories through interactive kiosks
to explore how you can get involved.</p>
<h4>Discover your Community Archetype!</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Our Network/Our Community Gallery/Archetypes.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p>At the end of your gallery journey, receive your unique community archetype
based on your RFID interactions. Each archetype reflects <strong>different ways of contributing</strong> to
Singapore's vibrant community spirit.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Planning Your Visit</strong>
</p>
<p>Allow <strong>approximately 60 minutes</strong> to fully experience all
zones. Check out <a href="https://www.pa.gov.sg/our-network/our-community-gallery/visitor-information" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Visitor Information</a> for
more details.</p>
