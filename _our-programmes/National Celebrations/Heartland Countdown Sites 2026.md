<p></p><a class="isomer-image-wrapper" href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-webimagecd2026"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="countdown 2026 sites" src="/images/Countdown_2026_KV.jpg"></a>
<p>Create shared memories and usher in the New Year with celebrations, fireworks
displays and drone shows, at various countdown celebration sites island
wide on 31 December 2025.</p>
<p>Residents can look forward to an exciting line-up of programmes to ring
in the New Year with family, friends and neighbours as one community close
to home at these 7 heartland countdown sites:&nbsp;</p>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-blcd2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Hearts @ Boon Lay, Building Brighter Futures!</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s): </strong>Boon Lay GROs</p>
<p><strong>Date: </strong>31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 7pm to 12.30am</p>
<p><strong>Venue: </strong>Hardcourt in front of Blk 215 Boon Lay Place</p>
<p>Residents can look forward to an unforgettable, high-energy celebration
bursting with music, dance, and meaningful moments that bring the whole
community together. Step onto our “Eras” Dance Floor, where the DJ will
spin multilingual hits from the groovy 60s all the way to today’s chart-toppers,
with the playlist curated by residents themselves through pre-event voting!</p>
<p>To make the celebration truly inclusive, student ushers from River Valley
High School(RVHS) will accompany and assist mobility-challenged seniors
so everyone can join in the fun. Adding to the festive charm, the Boon
Lay Youth Network and RVHS students will host nostalgic game booths inspired
by treasured cultural heritage, featuring timeless games and delightful
handicraft activities for all ages.</p>
<p>Families can also enjoy a heartwarming moment at the Tree of Gratitude,
where children are invited to pen heartfelt messages of appreciation to
their parents. The stage will come alive with vibrant live performances
by CODE:US, Flaming Matrix, Little Band Plus, and DJ Joshua.</p>
<p>And as the night reaches its peak, residents will countdown to the New
Year with a dazzling fireworks display that lights up the sky.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-khcountdown2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Keat Hong Countdown to 2026: All To Gather Party</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s): </strong>Keat Hong GROs</p>
<p><strong>Date: </strong>31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 8pm to 12.15am</p>
<p><strong>Venue: </strong>Keat Hong Square – Hardcourt next to Keat Hong
Community Club</p>
<p>Keat Hong countdown celebration will blend inclusivity, creativity and
innovation under Keat Hong's "All To Gather" theme. "All To Gather" embodies
the spirit of a countdown party that brings together Keat Hong's cherished
2025 flashbacks with hopeful new year dreams for 2026. The celebration
features a scene-by-scene reflection of the year's memorable moments. Community
Volunteers are also part of these reflections, helping to nurture the dreams
and aspirations of our residents.</p>
<p>The celebration will also feature the Recycled Fashion Showcase, now in
its third year, where cancer survivors will model sustainable outfits crafted
from recycled materials. Families with children can enjoy bouncy castles,
a choo-choo train ride, and a variety of games and snacks.</p>
<p>Rounding off the night, residents can look forward to an AI-enhanced finale,
where an original theme song, composed with the help of artificial intelligence
by Keat Hong grassroot leader Dharma – accompanies the fireworks display.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-marscountdown2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Marsiling Countdown As One</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s): </strong>Marsiling GROs</p>
<p><strong>Date: </strong>31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 8pm to 12.15am</p>
<p><strong>Venue: </strong>Woodlands Secondary School</p>
<p>This year’s Marsiling Countdown celebration will feature exciting performances
from Mandopop star Boon Hui Lu and local favourite 53A, as well as a spectacular
fireworks display to usher in 2026.</p>
<p>Residents can also look forward to colouring on three large decorative
banners of 1.2m by 2m each representing elements of the Marsiling Pillar
– Home, Heart, Hope.</p>
<p>In addition, they will have the opportunity to pen their personal wishes
for 2026, contributing to a shared tapestry of hopes for both the new year
and the new Marsiling CC. This meaningful collection of well-wishes will
be brought over to the new Marsiling CC, which is slated to open in 2026.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-nscd2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Nee Soon Countdown 2026: Dare to Dream</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s): </strong>Nee Soon GROs</p>
<p><strong>Date: </strong>31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 8pm to 12.30am</p>
<p><strong>Venue: </strong>Open field next to FutsalArena @ Yishun<strong> </strong>
</p>
<p>Residents can look forward to shopping at the Nee Soon Youth Market which
features over 50 young vendors turning their hobbies into entrepreneurial
ventures. Residents can also take part in Postcard to My Future Self where
they can pen messages of hope on</p>
<p>winning postcard designs that will be mailed back next year as reminders
of the dreams they dared to chase.</p>
<p>At the Art in the Heartlands corner, residents can create glow-in-the-dark
crafts and personalised keepsakes to bring home.</p>
<p>There will also be live performances by Ben Hum and Estelle Fly, Sharon
Shobana, Sarah Syazlina, and Shirlyn + The UnXpected, as the community
welcomes 2026 with music, art, youth expression and a lively carnival of
rides, snacks and games. The night will</p>
<p>culminate in a dazzling fireworks display.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-pungcd2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>One Punggol Year-End Countdown 2026</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s): </strong>One Punggol</p>
<p><strong>Date: </strong>31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 6pm to 12.30am</p>
<p><strong>Venue: </strong>One Punggol – Celebration Square and The Plaza/
Open Field in front of One Punggol</p>
<p>Residents can look forward to multiple themed zones designed for different
interests and age groups with carnival games, bouncy castles, photo booth
and more. Community talents will take centre stage with a vibrant line-up
of performances, including harmonica ensembles, karate demonstrations,
spoken poetry, ballet showcases, traditional Aura Gemalai, ukulele and
choir performances.</p>
<p>The night will culminate in a spectacular countdown experience featuring
a drone show and a fireworks display, accompanied by thrilling fire dancing,
fire eating and a high energy fire circus performance.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-othcd2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>OTH Countdown Celebrations to 2026</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s):</strong> Our Tampines Hub</p>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> 31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 12pm to 12.15am</p>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> Our Tampines Hub – Town Square Level 1, Festive
Area, Eco-Community Garden, Function Room 2, Community Auditorium and Central
Plaza</p>
<p>Our Tampines Hub (OTH) will celebrate the new year with a wide range of
family-friendly activities and a vibrant countdown concert with performing
artistes, culminating in a dazzling fireworks display to ring in 2026.</p>
<p>Families can enjoy Pororo Kids Carnival at Festive Walk East where children
can participate in bookmark-making activities, meet Pororo characters and
OTH Kids Club mascots, and bounce on a Pororo-themed inflatable.</p>
<p>At OTH Harvest Experience, families and friends can harvest community-grown
produce together. Residents can join Gardening Workshops covering basic
bonsai and air plant terrariums, as well as eco workshops that repurpose
used textiles and plastic bottles.</p>
<p>Residents of all ages can participate in fitness activities at the Community
Auditorium, including Step HIIT classes and parent-child tele-matches.
Central Plaza will be transformed into a digital playground with e-sports
try-outs and games for families to</p>
<p>enjoy.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/pa-wlcd2026" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Woodlands Waterfront Countdown 2026</u></a></strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p><strong>Organiser(s):</strong> Woodlands GROs</p>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> 31 Dec 2025</p>
<p><strong>Time:</strong> 9pm to 12.15am</p>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> Woodlands Waterfront Park&nbsp;</p>
<p>Residents can look forward to welcoming 2026 at the inaugural Woodlands
Waterfront Countdown, the first-ever New Year's Eve celebration at Woodlands
Waterfront Park. With its scenic coastal backdrop and free admission, the
event will promise a vibrant night of music, activities, and community
spirit.</p>
<p>A key highlight this year will be Sober Clubbing, bringing the globally
trending wellness movement to the heartlands. Residents of all ages will
enjoy an energetic, alcohol-free party experience with dynamic DJ sets,
including a headline performance by DJ Ezra</p>
<p>Hazard, while home-based businesses will serve mocktails, brewed coffee,
and creative snacks.</p>
<p>Families will also enjoy a lively carnival zone with inflatables, a pirate
ship ride, carnival games, and snack booths. The celebration will continue
with "Waves of Celebration," where Woodlands residents from youth to seniors
will take the Main Stage, showcasing</p>
<p>community talent from dancers and singers to percussionists.</p>
<p>The night will also feature a dazzling drone show over the waterfront,
adding a stunning visual element to the festivities.</p>
<p>Free shuttle buses from Woodlands North Plaza and Admiralty MRT Bus Stop
will ensure everyone can easily join this memorable New Year's Eve celebration.</p>
</div>
</details>
